# T4Agents 🇨🇦  
### Canadian T4 Payroll Intelligence Platform

T4Agents is a modern, developer-first application designed to structure, validate, and manage Canadian **T4 payroll reporting** with clarity and automation.

Built for small businesses, accountants, and developers who want clean payroll data models — not messy spreadsheets.

---

## 🇨🇦 What is a T4?

A **T4 – Statement of Remuneration Paid** is the official tax slip Canadian employers must issue to employees and submit to the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA).

It reports:

- Employment income  
- CPP contributions  
- EI contributions  
- Income tax deducted  
- Taxable benefits  
- Pension adjustments  
- Other compensation amounts  

T4Agents focuses on making this data structured, validated, and programmatically reliable.
